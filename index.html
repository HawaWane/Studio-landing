About Me

I'm Hawa Wane, 21, and photography has been my passion for a year now, a precious gift passed down from my mother. She instilled in me a love for capturing the world, inspired by travel and reading. Through my lens, I seek to freeze fleeting moments, explore unseen angles, and narrate silent stories. Reading enriches my perspective and broadens my horizons. The stories I consume blend with the scenes I capture, creating a tapestry of imagination and reality. My camera is my constant companion, allowing me to express what words cannot and to connect deeply with the environment around me. Photography is not just about taking pictures for me; it is a way of living, feeling, and sharing my view of the world. It is a continuous quest for beauty in the mundane, a means to immortalize memories, and a way to communicate emotions.
